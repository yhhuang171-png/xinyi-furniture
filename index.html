Company Profile

A furniture partner for global buyers.

We serve importers, distributors, retailers, project buyers and brand owners with production-ready furniture solutions.

English

Huizhou Xin Yi Furniture Co., Ltd. is a well-known furniture enterprise specializing in foreign trade exports, integrating R&D, production and sales. Established in 2003, we operate a self-owned factory covering approximately 30,000 square meters with about 200 staff members.

Our production range covers hardware structures, metal frames, stone and glass tabletops, upholstery and finished furniture for dining rooms, living rooms, bedrooms and commercial interiors.

中文

惠州新镒家具有限公司成立于2003年，是一家专注外贸出口的家具企业，集研发、生产、销售于一体。公司拥有约30000平方米自有工厂及约200名员工。
